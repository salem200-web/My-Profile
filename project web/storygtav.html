GTA V Full Story

Details of Characters, Missions, and Endings

📜 Story Introduction

The game starts in 2004 in North Yankton, where three criminals (Michael, Trevor, and Brad) pull off a major bank heist that goes disastrously wrong.
Nine years later, the story moves to Los Santos, where the lives of Michael, Franklin, and Trevor intertwine in a city full of crime, betrayal, and high-profile heists.

🧍‍♂️ Main Characters

1. Michael De Santa

A retired bank robber living a luxurious life with his family, dragged back into crime due to personal and financial struggles.

2. Franklin Clinton

A young ambitious hustler trying to rise above street life and become a professional criminal.

3. Trevor Philips

An unpredictable and violent criminal, Michael's old friend, who believes Michael is dead and runs his own criminal empire.

🗂️ Mission Count

🔹 Main Missions: Around 69 core missions driving the story forward.
🔹 Side Missions: Over 50 “Strangers & Freaks” missions with unique characters.
🔹 Random Events & Activities: Racing, parachuting, hunting, and other open-world events.

🧠 Mission Distribution by Character

🟡 Michael — approx. 44–69 missions (most in the story)
🔵 Franklin — approx. 39–59 missions
🔴 Trevor — approx. 31–58 missions

🔥 Story Endings

At the end of the game, players choose from three endings:
  • A. Kill Trevor
  • B. Kill Michael
  • C. Option Three: Save everyone and keep all three characters alive
Most players consider Option C as the “true ending” because all three survive and remain united.

📌 Playtime & Tips

Focusing only on the main story: ~20–30 hours of gameplay.
Completing all side missions and activities: 80+ hours of gameplay.
